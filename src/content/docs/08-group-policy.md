## In This Module

- Understand what Group Policy Objects (GPOs) are and how they apply
- Create a GPO that sets a desktop wallpaper (a fun, instantly visible result)
- Create a GPO that locks user screens after 10 minutes of inactivity (a real security control)
- Apply the GPOs and verify them with `gpupdate` and `gpresult`
